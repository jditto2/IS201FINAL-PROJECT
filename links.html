  1. Introduction to 3D Printing
    • Definition: 3D printing, or additive manufacturing, is a process of making three dimensional solid objects from a digital file.
    • History: The first 3D printing process was invented in the 1980s.
  2. Types of 3D Printing Technologies
    • Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)
    • Stereolithography (SLA)
    • Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)
  3. Materials Used in 3D Printing
    • Plastics: ABS, PLA, PVA, etc.
    • Metals: Steel, Aluminum, Titanium, etc.
    • Ceramics
  4. Applications of 3D Printing
    • Prototyping
    • Manufacturing
    • Medical and Dental
    • Construction
  5. Future of 3D Printing
    • Advancements in technology and materials
    • Increased accessibility
    • Potential challenges
